What is Alpine Ski Racing?

Alpine ski racing is a thrilling winter sport where athletes race down snow-covered slopes, navigating a series of gates as quickly as possible. It combines speed, precision, and technical skill, making it one of the most exciting disciplines in winter sports.

Types of Alpine Ski Racing

  1. Speed Events
    • Downhill
    • Super-G
  2. Technical Events
    • Slalom
    • Giant Slalom

History of Alpine Ski Racing

Alpine ski racing originated in the European Alps in the early 20th century. It has since grown into a globally celebrated sport, featured in the Winter Olympics and World Cup circuits.
